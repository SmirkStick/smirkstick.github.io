Folklore Hunter Creature Vote

As a thank you from the team at Liquid Donkey Games, we want to give you, the player, the ability to vote on the third hunt in Folklore Hunter.

We've had the Wendigo in 'Night of the Wendigo' and the Vampiric Strigoi in 'Bloodfang Forest'. Now, we want to hear what you think the third cryptid should be.

We've compiled a list of three of the most commonly suggested cryptids that we feel would be a good fit for the game. We've decided to exclude a few cryptids that are the primary focus of other recent games similar to Folklore Hunter (namely, Bigfoot/Yeti and the Skinwalker).
